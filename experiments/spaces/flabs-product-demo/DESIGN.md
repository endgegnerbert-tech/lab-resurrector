## Style Prompt

Dark technical product demo for flabs, an AI physics playground. The video should feel like a live lab UI and hackathon pitch at once: crisp browser panels, glowing simulation traces, agent tool calls, and measured data readouts. Motion is energetic but readable, with GSAP-driven entrances, wipes, particle bursts, slider changes, and simulated builds.

## Colors

- Background: #0d0d14
- Surface: #16161f
- Card: #1c1c28
- Accent: #7c6cf0
- Accent 2: #40d9b8
- Text: #e2e2ee
- Dim text: #7777aa

## Typography

- Body and UI: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", sans-serif
- Data, code, measurements: "JetBrains Mono", "SFMono-Regular", Consolas, monospace

## What NOT to Do

- Do not make it a landing page; show the working product and live simulations.
- Do not use unrelated stock footage or generic template scenes.
- Do not hide the agent behind vague AI copy; show tool calls and parameter changes.
- Do not drift away from the real flabs architecture: browser, Node, WebSocket, pi Agent, spaces.
